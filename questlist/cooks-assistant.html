Cook's Assistant

Description

The Lumbridge Castle Cook is in a mess. Today is the Duke of Lumbridge's birthday and the Cook is in charge of baking him a birthday cake. However, the Cook needs a lot of ingredients but he has forgotten to buy any of them; he'll never get them in time now. What is he going to do? Will you, the adventurer, help him in his predicament?

Required Items

  • Egg
  • Bucket of milk
  • Pot of flour
